ABOUT US

VCARE.COM : WE CARES FOR YOUR HAPPINESS.

Vcare.com is working as Non Governmental Organisation, or NGO. We are involved in carrying out a wide range of activities related to Media literacy for the benefit of underprivileged people and the society at large. We at Vcare.com aim to spread awareness of Media Literacy among the whole society. We target to involve those people who are unaware about the uses of media and outcomes related to it.

We provide an opportunity to those who want to go inside the depth of today's digital age, who want to learn about the uses media and want to become media literate. Vcare.com provides information to them regarding the proper uses of media, the upcomings, the advantages, the disadvantages, how to use it and how to react on it, what is safe and what's not...all these necessary informations related to today's era. We think that it is important for a person to be aware regarding all these factors because in today's life everything is digital. For even more development and enhancement of Vcare.com and people, we are running campaigns. Our Successful campaigns establish credibility and a sense of truth and rightness in all that they do. We use campaigns to mobilise and involve people in our work. We also use campaigns to pressurise decision-makers, to educate the public and to make them media literate.

Hence, we at Vcare.com are trying to cover most of the population in the field of media literate person so that our population acts as an asset for our economy as well as they can protect themselves from cyber related issues and frauds. We have taken the initiative of encouraging more and more people in this field of awareness as well as media literacy. Be encouraged, be awared and be media literate!
