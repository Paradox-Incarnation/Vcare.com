Let's Know About Media Literacy

A PROVISION TO UNDERSTAND DIGITAL WORLD

Media literacy holds within the practices that allow people to have data access (the ability to get what you need), critical evaluation, and design or operate media. Media literacy is not restricted to one medium.

Therefore, we as the readers or viewers need to view the media impartially, with the goal to find out or analyze what is being presented.

Why Media Literacy?

In today's digital age, Media Literacy is an essential skill for everyone. Due to the following reasons:

1. It develops the mind for criticized thinking - Media literacy encourages critical thinking and self-expression, enabling citizens to decisively exercise their democratic rights. People who are media literate can adopt a critical stance when decoding media messages, no matter their views regarding a position.


2. Consume the information smartly and sharply - It is necessary to consume the information smartly and sharply because it is not necessary that every information provided is true or false. Media literacy helps to learn how to determine whether something is credible. Basically, it makes the readers or viewers think about any information very intentively and aware them regarding the consequences of fake or unsecure news as they may take up your whole data.


3. Identification of veiwpoint - Media literacy encourages critical thinking and self-expression, enabling citizens to decisively exercise their democratic rights. People who are media literate can adopt a critical stance when decoding media messages, no matter their views regarding a position.


4. Responsible designation of media - It states that one should create media responsibly and should be totally aware about the proper designation of media. One must recognize the point of view that the person wants to state as if what you want to say, how you want to stay and the way in which you will say or convey your thoughts, messages or your own point of view.


5. Recognization of media's role - It is very important to identify or recognize the role of media in our culture as media provides us various information out of which most of them are important and have an impact in our day to day life. From celebrity gossip to magazine covers to memes, media is telling us something, shaping our understanding of the world, and even compelling us to act or think in certain ways.


6. Understand the goals of developer - What does the author want you to take away from a piece of media? Is it purely informative, is it trying to change your mind, or is it introducing you to new ideas you've never heard of? When kids understand what type of influence something has, they can make informed choices.
