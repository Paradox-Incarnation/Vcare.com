CYBER CRIME : A DIGITAL CRIME

Cybercrime is a crime that involves a computer and a network. The computer may have been used in the commission of a crime, or it may be the target. Cybercrime may harm someone's security and financial health. Cybercrime that targets computers often involves viruses and other types of malware.

Cybercrime that uses computers to commit other crimes may involve using computers or networks to spread malware, illegal information or illegal images. Sometimes cybercriminals conduct both categories of cybercrime at once.

In simple, we can say it as criminal activities carried out by means of computers or the internet.

Types of Cyber Crime :

1. Identity Theft - This cybercrime occurs when a criminal gains access to a user’s personal information to steal funds, access confidential information, or participate in tax or health insurance fraud. They can also open a phone/internet account in your name, use your name to plan a criminal activity and claim government benefits in your name.

2. Cyberstalking - This kind of cybercrime involves online harassment where the user is subjected to a plethora of online messages and emails. Typically cyberstalkers use social media, websites and search engines to intimidate a user and instill fear. This practice is growing continuously...

3. Theft of financial or card payment data - Credit card fraud is an inclusive term for fraud committed using a payment card, such as a credit card or debit card. The purpose may be to obtain goods or services, or to make payment to another account which is controlled by a criminal. 

4. Email and internet fraud - This act is of sending an e-mail falsely claiming to be an established legitimate business in an attempt to deceive the unsuspecting recipient into divulging personal, sensitive information such as passwords, credit card numbers, and bank account information after directing the user to visit a specified website.

As technology advances and more people rely on the internet to store sensitive information such as banking or credit card information, criminals increasingly attempt to steal that information.

Cybercrime is becoming more of a threat to people across the world. Raising awareness about how information is being protected and the tactics criminals use to steal that information continues to grow in importance.

Following tips will help you to be protected from Cyber Crime...

  • Creation of strong passwords is must
  • Software and operating system should be updated
  • Usage of anti - virus software
  • Never open attachments in spam mails
  • Never click spam links, websites or mails
  • Always keep an eye on your bank statements
  • Never share any of your personal data until you verify!
  • Do not trust spam messages
  • Trust only the trusted sites

It is necessary for a media literate person to know regarding the steps to be taken after cyber attack.

Hence, follow these simple 6 steps immediately after facing cyber attack :

  • Disconnect your internet.
  • Disable remote access.
  • Maintain your firewall settings.
  • Immediately install any pending security updates.
  • Change your passwords and recreate strong ones
  • Inform the cyber cell nearby you.

Follow these important tips and make others aware regarding this common crime which takes up your whole data. So, be protected from such cyber issues by becoming even more careful, responsible and aware...
